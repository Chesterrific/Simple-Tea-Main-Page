Returns

As tea is treated as a food item by the USFDA, returns are not accepted (food safety risk).

Upon receiving your order, if you find the package is damaged or an error in your order, please contact our customer services at contact@simpletea.com within 7 days of delivery. Please attach a photograph or the damaged or incorrect item with your order number.

We’ll do our best to keep you very happy.
